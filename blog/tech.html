Spacing

1. What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

  • Margin:

    In general, the elements in a webpage are boxes and margin is the property that position it based on its surroundings. In other words, margin is the property that sets the distance in-between an element and other external elements around it.

  • Border:

    Border is the property that separates the internal content from the external content of an element.

  • Padding:

    Padding as opposed to margin sets the position of the internal content in relation to its border. In other words, padding sets the internal position of the content of an element while margin sets the position of an element around other external elements.
