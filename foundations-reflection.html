What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

  • Manage Frustrations:

    I noticed that sometimes the code won't work as I expect and it is fine. Every single programming language has got some peculiarities and it will sometimes have some behavior that I don't expect and it's alright because instead of causing me frustrations I should actually take it as a opportunity to learn one new thing. And the programer journey is all about learning so if I get frustrated with having to learn something new, I am probably at the wrong place. So, I will try to take not working code as a learning opportunity instead of getting frustrated.

  • Reflecting is the way to learn:

    It was a big surprise for me that learning is more about reflecting about what you learned, which is pretty much thinking about what went well and what did not go that well. Reflecting helps your brain to keep the information.

  • Teaching helps you to learn:

    Apparently, explaining someone else what you know helps you to find out what are the gaps on your own knowledge. If you cannot make someone understand something probably you do not fully understand about that specific topic. So sharing your knowledge helps you to learn more, so the idea that everybody is a teacher and a student at the same time guides this philosophy.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

  • Values:

    You values in both learning and programming as actually in anything in a person's life guides how the person will behave in each circumstances. For instance, if a person has the value of a growth mindset person this person will often be more optimist about challenges him or her finds on the way. A person with a very strong ethical value will behave in a certain way. And any institution or organisation, whether it is work related or not, usually determines the values they are looking for and obviously they want to gather people who follow those values. And that's the importance of a person defining what their values are because then this person can more easily find the places which will be a better match for him/her.

  • Empathy:

    Empathy is a very beautiful value because it brings the humanity into the game. The power of being able to put yourself on someone else's shoes is a great advantage when working in a team. People will feel more embraced when in an empathic environment and more likely to feel motivated, consequently enhancing personal performance. And it's not only about personal performance but also the idea of working as a team. If a person feels they are well understood by their peers and bosses it's more likely the interaction and deliverables of this group of people achieve higher quality standards, decreasing as a consequence communication problems.

  • Self-Awareness:

    Self-awareness plays an important role on a person's life because it's everyone's responsibility to understand how they feel on each situation and as a consequence how they react because if you are aware of what triggers each emotion you are more likely to be able to manage them.

    At the end of the day, every person is responsible for how they react and how they interact with other people and it will define their values, obviously and therefore what group of people this person will be more suitable to work with.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

The idea of neuroplasticity was something interesting that I actually have already applied but I hadn't explored much. There are a few skills I have developed by working really hard, but I strongly believe these are not a talents I have but passions which drove me to practice them day after day, almost an obsession, so I ended up learning. And it is all about neuroplasticity. The fact we are able to develop skills we did not have before. And it is so empowering because it gives people hope, the hope they need to fight for something they want. The hope will drive them to achieve what they strongly desire.

Also the fact that reflecting and teaching are great ways of learning were also a surprise for me. I had not thought about it before. The importance of sharing knowledge on a person's learning journey. And it is actually a beautiful advantage because it promotes people helping each other and it makes us more human, I believe. The sense of community.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

No doubt the fact of writing down my reflections is one of the biggest challenges for me. I am definitely not a person who can easily translate my thoughts into words and write it down. Expressing my feelings in words is a big challenge but as discussed in the neuroplasticity it is something I can learn. If I keep practicing and include it in my habits one day it will feel way more natural than it does today.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

I believe it is because Dev Academy understands the importance of human skills for a person's success in any department of their lives. Even if Dev Academy was not in the Tech Industry I still believe the institution would still have a strong focus on human development and it is because it is important no matter where you are.

Human skills are not only needed in the Tech Industry, it is needed in any job or anywhere you have human interaction. Technical skills can make a of any professional a great professional, however only the human skills can bring the same person to an outstanding level.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead? Justify your answer.

Learning or polishing the human skills' knowledge is never a waste of time. It is great DEA has it on the curriculum because people that have not explored these topics before have now the opportunity to hear about them and go deeper if they want and also people who have explored them have the chance to refresh them.

Another advantage is that people on different levels can work together and share knowledge which makes experienced people get even greater on it and also not very experienced people started their learning journey on core values.
